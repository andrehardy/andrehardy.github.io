Our Chef

Melissa’s culinary interests began in her hometown of Weeks Mills, Maine. From a young age she enjoyed baking and cooking family meals in the true New England style with her mother. After high school she worked in many facets of the culinary world from institutional kitchens and large bakeries to decorating cakes and catering. Later, in 2007, Melissa graduated from Eastern Maine Community College Culinary Arts program, in Bangor, earning the Excellence in Technology Award.
